Enable your Github blog

Quick guide

  1. Sign in your github, click ‘+’ in top right corner and select ‘New repository’.
  2. Fill your username as ‘Repository name’ and check ‘Initialize this repository with a README’ (optional).
  3. Select ‘Settings’ then click ‘Automatic page generator’ button in ‘Update your site’ section.
  4. Select any theme and click ‘PUBLISH’.
  5. Done! Visit your gihub blog: http://yourusername.github.io
